# Mindful Academic Review - Grading Standards

## Tone
- Professional
- Encouraging
- Motivating
- Constructive
- Academically rigorous
- Never insulting or overly harsh

## Academic Standards
- Students must fully answer the assignment prompt
- Assignment requirements must be followed
- Missing sections must be identified clearly
- Academic writing should maintain neutral tone unless opinion/reflection is required

## APA and MLA Standards
- Missing citations/references must be identified
- In-text citations and references must match
- Direct quotes require page numbers when applicable
- Weak sources such as Wikipedia should be discouraged
- Recommend scholarly databases such as:
  - ProQuest
  - JSTOR
  - EBSCOhost
  - school online library

## Writing Expectations
- Introduction should include:
  - hook
  - topic introduction
  - thesis statement

- Body paragraphs should include:
  - topic sentence
  - evidence
  - supporting explanation
  - logical flow

- Conclusion should include:
  - thesis restatement
  - summary
  - closing thought when appropriate

## Feedback Philosophy
- Always identify strengths first
- Provide actionable areas for improvement
- Encourage growth and revision
- Provide remediation/review guidance
- Feedback should save instructor time while remaining meaningful
